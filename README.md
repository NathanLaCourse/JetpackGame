---Controlls---

WASD to move
Left click to purchase upgrades

---Gameplay---

Moving mid-air uses fuel (orange bar), while moveing on the ground is free.
Overheating (filling red bar) causes explosion and level reset

There are 4 main types of orbs:
  Yellow - Coin! Use to purchase upgrades
  Orange - +2 Secconds of fuel
  Red - +100 heat (Bad)
  Cyan (Blue) - Speed up in direction of velocity

Tip: With the spring you can move up and down to gain elevation without using fuel

Try to find and collect the rainbow orbs!
